Using Obsidian for Knowledge Management

#obsidian #note-taking #knowledge-management

Obsidian is a powerful note-taking app that creates a connected web of your thoughts using bi-directional links. It stores notes as plain markdown files on your local device, giving you complete control over your data. The graph view visualizes connections between your notes, helping you discover unexpected relationships in your knowledge base. Whether you’re a researcher, student, or professional, Obsidian transforms how you capture and connect ideas.
